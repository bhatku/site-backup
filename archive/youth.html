Youth Retreat - Tune into Nature

Age Group: 18-35
Upcoming Dates:
APril/June 2019 , click to register.

About the retreat:
Life has us hen-pecked on most days. With to do lists, don't lists, rat races, daily chases.
It's time to take a pause, don't you think? To make sense of everything around us and within us.

Detox, reflect & re-tune yourself.

Who is it for?
If you are curious about music and nature - this is for you.

If you are looking to slow down, take a step forward and open yourself up for new experiences, this retreat is for you. We are looking for participants who wish to understand nature and all that it has to offer, people who are open to go back to simpler living habits and explore their response to such surroundings. The retreat holds space for everyone with an open mind and the will to explore.

What can you expect?
On a personal level, we wish for you to open up and have conversations about your life journeys, the meaning you find in the mundane and what makes you happy. We are looking to have discussions on health and how to find a balance between the various aspects of our lives. We will all cook and work on the farm together to get a holistic experience of living close to the earth.


Moreover, you can also look forward to a release of feel good, happy hormones as you eat freshly made dinner under the starlit skies.
